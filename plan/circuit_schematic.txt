================================================================================
TM4C123GXL HEADER PIN LOCATIONS
================================================================================

  The LaunchPad has two rows of headers: J1 (left) and J3/J4 (right).

  J1 Header (left side of board, top to bottom):
    J1.01 = +3.3V
    J1.02 = PB5
    J1.03 = PB0
    J1.04 = PB1
    J1.05 = PE4
    J1.06 = PE5
    J1.07 = PB4    <-- NOTE: NOT PB6! See below.
    J1.08 = PA5    <-- NOTE: NOT PB7! See below.
    J1.09 = PA6
    J1.10 = PA7

  J2 Header (left side, bottom):
    J2.01 = GND
    J2.02 = PB2
    J2.03 = PE0
    J2.04 = PF0
    J2.05 = RESET
    J2.06 = PB7 (T0CCP1) ***  <-- IR LED DRIVER OUTPUT
    J2.07 = PB6 (T0CCP0) ***  <-- TSOP INPUT
    J2.08 = PA4
    J2.09 = PA3
    J2.10 = PA2

  J3 Header (right side, top):
    J3.01 = +5V    *** POWER for TSOP and LED circuit
    J3.02 = GND    *** GROUND for all external circuits
    J3.03 = PD0
    J3.04 = PD1
    J3.05 = PD2
    J3.06 = PD3
    J3.07 = PE1
    J3.08 = PE2
    J3.09 = PE3
    J3.10 = PF1

  IMPORTANT: PB6 and PB7 are on J2 (pins J2.07 and J2.06), NOT on J1!
  Verify your board's silkscreen. The pin numbers above are for the
  EK-TM4C123GXL Rev D LaunchPad. Check your specific board revision.


================================================================================
BREADBOARD LAYOUT GUIDE
================================================================================

  Suggested breadboard arrangement (view from top):

  +--[LaunchPad mounted across center channel]--+
  |                                              |
  |  LEFT SIDE (J1/J2 pins accessible)          |
  |  RIGHT SIDE (J3/J4 pins accessible)         |
  +----------------------------------------------+

  Power Rails:
    Top rail (+)  = +5V from J3.01
    Top rail (-)  = GND from J3.02
    Bottom rail   = same, jumped across

  TSOP area (right side of breadboard):
    Row A: 100 Ohm from +5V rail to TSOP pin 3
    Row B: 4.7 uF cap from TSOP pin 3 to TSOP pin 2
    Row C: TSOP pin 2 to GND rail
    Row D: TSOP pin 1 to wire going to J2.07 (PB6)

  LED Driver area (left side of breadboard):
    Row E: 47 Ohm from +5V rail to IR333-A anode
    Row F: IR333-A cathode to 2N3904 collector
    Row G: 2N3904 emitter to GND rail
    Row H: 1 kOhm from J2.06 (PB7) to 2N3904 base
    Row I: 10 kOhm from 2N3904 base to GND rail (pull-down)

================================================================================
ELECTRICAL VERIFICATION CALCULATIONS
================================================================================

  TSOP13438 Supply:
    Vs range (from datasheet): 2.5V to 5.5V
    With 100 Ohm and ~0.5mA supply current: drop = 0.05V
    Actual Vs = 5.0V - 0.05V = 4.95V  (within range)

  IR LED Current:
    I_LED = (V_supply - V_LED - V_CE(sat)) / R
    I_LED = (5.0 - 1.2 - 0.2) / 47 = 76.6 mA peak
    At 1/3 duty (38 kHz carrier): I_avg = 25.5 mA
    IR333-A max continuous: 100 mA  --> OK
    IR333-A max peak (at 1/3 duty): 200 mA --> OK (76.6 << 200)

  2N3904 Base Drive:
    I_base = (V_PB7 - V_BE) / R_base
    I_base = (3.3V - 0.7V) / 1000 = 2.6 mA
    Required for saturation: I_C / hFE = 76.6 / 100 = 0.77 mA
    Overdrive factor: 2.6 / 0.77 = 3.4x --> Well into saturation, OK

  Base Pull-Down (10 kOhm):
    When PB7 is driven HIGH: voltage divider 1k/(1k+10k) is negligible.
    Base voltage = 3.3V × 10k/(1k+10k) = 3.0V, still well above 0.7V Vbe --> OK
    During reset (PB7 floating): 10 kOhm pulls base to GND --> transistor OFF

  TM4C PB7 Source Current:
    2.6 mA base drive << 8 mA max GPIO source current --> OK

  PB6 Input (5V tolerant):
    TSOP output: 0V (low) to ~4.95V (high)
    PB6 is 5V tolerant per TM4C123GH6PM datasheet table 23-5 --> OK
